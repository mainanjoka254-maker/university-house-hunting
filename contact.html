Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about UniHouse Kenya

Simply click on "Login / Register" at the top of the page, select "Student" as your role, fill in your details, and create an account. Once registered, you can browse houses, save favorites, and contact landlords.

Register as a "Landlord", then log in and click "Post House" from your dashboard. Fill in the property details including price, location, amenities, and photos. Your listing will be visible to students searching near the selected university.

Registration and browsing are completely free for both students and landlords. There are no listing fees or hidden charges. We believe in making student accommodation accessible to everyone.

Open the listing you're interested in, scroll down to the "Contact Landlord" form on the right side, fill in your name, email, and message, then click "Send Inquiry". The landlord will receive your message and contact you directly.
